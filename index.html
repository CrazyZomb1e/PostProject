About UNCP

A university shaped by history, service and student success.

Mission

UNCP emphasizes excellence in teaching and learning across bachelor's, master's and professional doctoral programs while encouraging inquiry, collaboration and strong intellectual standards.

History

Established in 1887 to educate American Indian teachers, UNC Pembroke has grown into a public university serving students from many backgrounds while continuing to honor its heritage.

Identity

The university presents itself as personal, affordable and student-centered, with a strong commitment to regional service, diversity and preparation for leadership in a changing world.

FAQ

Common questions from new and returning students.

Where is UNC Pembroke located?

UNCP is located in Pembroke, North Carolina, at 1 University Drive, Pembroke, NC 28372-1510.

What is UNCP known for?

Its official site emphasizes personalized teaching, affordability, a diverse student body, strong campus support and deep regional history.

How did the university begin?

UNCP was established in 1887 and began as a school created to educate American Indian teachers.

What does campus life include?

According to UNCP, students can explore housing, clubs, recreation, athletics and a range of support resources designed to help them thrive.
